Oftentimes we’ll hear from our patients how they hadn’t seen a dentist in many years because they don’t have dental insurance or cannot afford it out-of-pocket. For those of you who may be in this situation, we’d like to welcome you back into the dental office! While visiting your dentist regularly may not give you a brighter smile right away, it will ensure that any cavities are caught early and tooth decay is treated before it reaches the point where a root canal is necessary. Dental cleanings can remove plaque and tartar buildup from around the gum line which causes bad breath and takes away from your overall smile. Gentle scraping of the teeth will also remove any old plaque or tartar that has been building up for years.
